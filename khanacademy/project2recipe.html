Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Step 2: Add milk, egg, and oil. Mix until smooth.
Step 3: Cook on a hot pan until golden on both sides.

Fresh Garden Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato 1
Cucumber 1
Lettuce 1
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Chop all vegetables.
Step 2: Mix olive oil, lemon juice, and salt.
Step 3: Toss everything together.
